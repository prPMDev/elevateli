Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2025
🔒 Your Privacy is Our Priority

Our Core Privacy Commitment

ElevateLI operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your LinkedIn profile data to our servers because we don't have any servers. Your data stays on your computer.

What Information We Access

ElevateLI only accesses information that is publicly visible on your LinkedIn profile page when you choose to analyze it:

  • Profile photo presence
  • Professional headline
  • About section
  • Work experience
  • Education
  • Skills
  • Recommendations
  • Certifications
  • Projects
  • Featured content

How We Use Your Information

The information accessed is used solely to:

  • Calculate completeness score: Performed entirely in your browser
  • Generate AI insights: Only if you enable AI and provide your own API key
  • Display recommendations: Shown only to you within the extension

Data Storage

What We Store Locally

  • Your LinkedIn profile URL: To identify your profile
  • Analysis cache: To avoid re-analyzing unchanged profiles
  • Your settings: AI preferences, target role, etc.
  • Encrypted API key: Only if you choose to enable AI features

Storage Security

All data is stored using Chrome's secure storage APIs:

  • API keys are encrypted using Web Crypto API
  • Data is isolated to your browser profile
  • No data is accessible to other extensions or websites

Third-Party Services

AI Analysis (Optional): If you enable AI features and provide an API key:

  • Profile text is sent directly from your browser to OpenAI's API
  • We never see or store your API key on our servers
  • AI providers process data according to their privacy policies
  • You can disable AI features at any time

Important: We do not have any servers or backend infrastructure. All AI requests go directly from your browser to the AI provider.

What We DON'T Do

  • ❌ Collect personal information
  • ❌ Track your browsing behavior
  • ❌ Store data on external servers
  • ❌ Share or sell any data
  • ❌ Use analytics or tracking pixels
  • ❌ Access private LinkedIn messages or connections
  • ❌ Modify your LinkedIn profile

Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Reset Analysis: Clear all cached analysis data
  • Reset Extension: Remove all stored data and settings
  • Disable AI: Turn off AI features at any time
  • Uninstall: Removes all extension data from your browser

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Open Source Transparency

ElevateLI is open source. You can review our entire codebase on GitHub to see exactly how we handle your data.
